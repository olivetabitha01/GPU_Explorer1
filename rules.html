Welcome, Explorer.

🎮 Game Structure
Level 1: CPU vs GPU — 5 quick questions. Discuss with your team and lock in answers.
Level 2: Hardware Puzzle — drag the GPU to its PCIe slot, then answer a component question.
Total Time Limit: 10 minutes for both levels combined.
The timer starts the moment you click Start Mission.
🏆 Scoring System
ActionPoints
Level 1 — Each correct answer+1 pt
Level 2 — GPU placed correctly+0.5 pt
Level 2 — Component MCQ correct+0.5 pt
Wrong answerNo penalty
Completed under 5 min+1 BONUS
TOTAL (max)7 pts
📊 Final Ranking Logic
First priority: Highest total score wins.
Tiebreaker: If scores are equal, the team with the lowest completion time ranks higher.
Team best: The best single run per team counts for leaderboard.
Judges add 0–4 bonus points for teamwork (observed separately).
👥 Team Rules
All members must be present and actively discussing.
Assign team roles before starting (see below).
The Leader clicks "Confirm Answer" — not just anyone.
No outside help, phones, or search engines.
⚖️ Judge Observation Criteria (Bonus 0–4 pts)
All team members visibly participating +1
Active discussion observed before answering +1
Smooth role coordination (no one dominating) +1
Clear leadership and composure under time pressure +1
🎭 Team Roles — Assign Before You Start

Each role is critical. Rotate if your team has fewer than 6.
